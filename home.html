Current Project

Rope Burn (later renamed Scurry)

In Development

Created with: Monogame

Description: Can you beat your opponent to the your place as a dojo master?

This game will require players to use their left and right keys to climb lines of rope to the segment that they think the dojo master wants. Be careful not to fall down as you try and figure out exactly what colour he means!

Intent: To make updates on some of my former university work based on new knowledge.
