About Me

Hello and welcome to my website!

For four years, after starting in 2014, I studied at Nottingham Trent University for a BSc(Hons) degree in Computer Science (Games Technology). In addition to my studies, I became a member of the university's development society (DevSoc), taking a Vice President role between 2015 and 2016. Representing this society, I had participated in multiple game jams and helped younger students in programming lessons at the National Videogame Arcade in Nottingham.

This has been part of long-lasting ambition of mine to be an active member of the computing industry, especially the gaming sector.

Recently my website, which acted as a portfolio of my work over the past few years, was closed down. I am currently in the process of re-uploading projects here. With that said, updates will be made to the site regularly, particularly in the next few weeks, cataloging old and new developments.

I hope that you find enjoyment in viewing my progress and let me know what you think of my work - I am always interested in constructive feedback that could help me to improve.

Thanks and kind regards.
